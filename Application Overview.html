KanTime is a cloud-based enterprise software solution designed to streamline operations for post-acute healthcare providers, including home health, hospice, pediatric, and personal care services. It offers end-to-end workflow automation that supports compliance, clinical documentation, scheduling, billing, and mobile access for field staff.

Targeted Users: KanTime is used by agency administrators, schedulers, nurses, therapists, billing teams, and compliance officers to deliver coordinated, high-quality patient care while maintaining operational excellence
