
#Overview#
KanTime is a cloud-based enterprise software solution designed to streamline operations for 
post-acute healthcare providers, including home health, hospice, pediatric, and personal care services. 
It offers end-to-end workflow automation that supports compliance, clinical documentation, 
scheduling, billing, and mobile access for field staff.

#Targeted Users#
KanTime is used by agency administrators, schedulers, nurses, therapists, billing teams, 
and compliance officers to deliver coordinated, high-quality patient care while 
maintaining operational excellence.

##Key benefits##
 1. ** Point-of-Care Documentation**: Clinicians can document patient visits in real-time 
 using KanTime's mobile-friendly interface, which supports both online and offline modes.
